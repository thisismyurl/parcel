What Makes It Different

🌿
Single-Estate Sourcing

Every bottle comes from one grove, one harvest, one producer. We know their names. We've been to the farm.

⏱
48-Hour Cold Infusion

Slow-roasted garlic, introduced at harvest temperature, never above 32°C. The result is mellow depth, not sharp bite.

🌎
No Additives, Ever

Cold-press only. No additives, no preservatives, no colour treatment. The label lists two ingredients: olive oil, garlic.

The Maker

Maker Portrait

Second-generation olive growers in the South Okanagan. They converted the family orchard to certified organic in 2011. The garlic oil is a side project that became their most requested product.
